I am an Assistant Professor in the Integrated Systems Engineering and Electrical and Computer Engineering Departments at The Ohio State University. I am also affiliated with the Translational Data Analytics Institute.

I study learning and decision making, particularly in network environments and when agents are rational, and examine how information and incentive design can influence agents' behavior in these environments. This research contributes to and draws from several disciplines, including network economics, game theory, algorithmic economics, optimization, and reinforcement learning. My research is generously supported by the National Science Foundation (NSF) and Amazon.
