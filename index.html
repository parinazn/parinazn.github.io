I am an Assistant Professor in the Integrated Systems Engineering and Electrical and Computer Engineering Departments at The Ohio State University. I am also affiliated with the Translational Data Analytics Institute.

My research interests are in network economics, game theory, algorithmic economics, and reinforcement learning. Specifically, my group studies decision making and learning in networks (network games and multi-agent reinforcement learning), fairness in AI (data biases and long-term impacts with human-in-the-loop), and economics of cyber security (incentive design and data analytics). Our group's research is generously supported by the National Science Foundation (NSF), Amazon, and Cisco.
